What is Intention?

The Intention Algorithm is an innovative cryptographic method, developed over five years, to recover lost Bitcoin private keys without brute force and without compromising decentralization. It introduces a new cryptographic paradigm: recovery through distributed inference based on intention.

Intention is not just a theory; it's a proven methodology that applies advanced cryptographic techniques, ensuring that a lost private key can be recovered without damaging the Bitcoin network's integrity.
