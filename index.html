What is Intention Project?

Project Intention is a cryptographic revolution that breaks everything you thought you knew about Bitcoin key security and loss. For over five years, our founders, Imalaca and EntropySeeker, have worked silently on an algorithm capable of the impossible: recovering lost private keys without brute force, without compromising the network, and without sacrificing decentralization.

Intention is not just a theory; it's a proven methodology that uses intentional distributed inference combined with mathematical projection maps and advanced probability models, restoring what once seemed irrecoverable with pinpoint precision.
