What is Project Intention?

Project Intention is a cryptographic revolution that breaks everything you thought you knew about security and key loss in Bitcoin. For over five years, our founders, Imalaca and EntropySeeker, have been silently working on an algorithm capable of the impossible: recovering lost private keys without brute force, without violating the network, and without compromising decentralization.

Intention is the first system in the world that doesn’t search for keys randomly. Instead, it utilizes an approach called Intentional Distributed Inference, which combines mathematical projection maps with advanced probability models. What once seemed unrecoverable, can now be restored with pinpoint precision.

How does it work?

While other methods rely on luck or computational power, Intention identifies natural patterns of intention in the creation of keys, using advanced mathematics to reduce a universe of possibilities to a high-probability focal point. When a user requests assistance, Intention does not explore randomly: it reconstructs the mental and mathematical map that led to the original creation of their private key, cross-referencing this data with a distributed model that operates in secure environments.

"Vuelta" Module: Protection Against Theft

What if someone steals your funds? Enter our “Vuelta” system.

Vuelta is a hidden protocol that can be embedded in any compatible wallet. In the event of unauthorized movement (e.g., if someone accesses your funds without your knowledge), it triggers a scheduled recovery sequence that redirects the funds to a new, secure address belonging to the true owner.

It’s like an immune system within your wallet: silent, vigilant, and ready to act only when needed.
