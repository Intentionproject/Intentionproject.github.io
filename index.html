What is Intention?

The Intention Algorithm is an innovative cryptographic method developed over five years to recover lost Bitcoin private keys without brute force and without compromising decentralization. It introduces a new cryptographic paradigm: recovery through distributed inference based on intent.

Intention is not just a theory; it's a proven methodology that uses advanced cryptographic techniques to ensure that a lost private key can be recovered without harming the integrity of the Bitcoin network.

How does it work?

Unlike methods that rely on chance or computational brute force, Intention identifies natural patterns of intent in key creation by using advanced mathematics to reduce an infinite number of possibilities to a high-probability focal point.

When a user requests help, Intention does not search randomly; it reconstructs the mental and mathematical map that led to the original creation of the private key, cross-referencing it with a distributed model operating in secure environments.

"Vuelta" Module: Protection Against Theft

What if someone steals your funds? This is where our “Vuelta” system comes in.

Vuelta is a hidden protocol that can be embedded in any compatible wallet. If an unauthorized movement is detected (for example, if someone accesses your funds without your knowledge), a programmed recovery sequence is triggered that redirects the funds to a new, secure address of the rightful owner.

It works like an immune system within your wallet: silent, vigilant, and ready to act only when needed.
